Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 10, 2026

1. Overview

ProposalKit ("we", "us", "the app") is a proposal builder application created by Delta System. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about what data we collect.

2. Data Storage

Local-Only Mode (Default)

By default, ProposalKit stores all data in your browser's localStorage. This means:

  • Your proposals, clients, settings, and templates stay on your device
  • No data is sent to our servers
  • Clearing your browser data will delete your ProposalKit data
  • Data is not accessible from other devices

Cloud Sync Mode (Opt-in)

If you create an account and enable cloud sync, your data is stored in a Supabase-hosted PostgreSQL database:

  • Your proposals, clients, settings, and templates are synced to the cloud
  • Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest
  • Row-level security ensures only you can access your data
  • You can delete your account and all cloud data at any time

3. Information We Collect

Account Information (Cloud Sync only)

  • Email address
  • Name (if provided)
  • Authentication data (managed by Supabase Auth)

Proposal Data

  • Proposal content, pricing, client details, and settings you enter
  • This data is only stored locally or in your cloud account — never shared with third parties

Analytics (if enabled)

We may use privacy-friendly analytics (Plausible) to understand how the app is used. This collects:

  • Page views and referrer data
  • No cookies, no personal data, no tracking across sites
  • Fully GDPR compliant

4. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not track your IP address for analytics
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties
  • We do not use advertising cookies or trackers
  • We do not read or access your proposal content

5. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — Cloud database and authentication (if you opt into cloud sync). Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Google OAuth — If you sign in with Google. Google Privacy Policy
  • Vercel — Hosting. Vercel Privacy Policy
  • CDN providers — jsDelivr, unpkg, Google Fonts for loading libraries and fonts

6. Shared Proposals

When you share a proposal via link, the recipient can view the proposal content. Shared proposals are accessible to anyone with the link. You can revoke access by regenerating the share token.

7. Data Retention

  • Local mode: Data persists until you clear your browser data or uninstall
  • Cloud mode: Data persists until you delete your account
  • We do not retain data after account deletion

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all your data (it's visible in the app)
  • Export your data (via the app's export features)
  • Delete your data (clear localStorage or delete your cloud account)
  • Use the app without creating an account

9. Children's Privacy

ProposalKit is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
