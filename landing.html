FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is ProposalKit really free? ▾
Yes. The Free plan includes unlimited proposals, all 13 PDF templates, client management, payment tracking, analytics, and offline support. No signup required, no credit card, no catch.
Where is my data stored? ▾
All data is stored locally in your browser using LocalStorage. Nothing is sent to any server. You own your data completely. You can export/import your data as JSON at any time from Settings.
Does it work offline? ▾
Yes. ProposalKit is a Progressive Web App (PWA) with full offline support. You can create, edit, and export proposals without an internet connection. Install it to your home screen for native-like experience.
Can clients sign proposals digitally? ▾
Yes. When you share a proposal via secure link, clients can view it in a beautiful portal and accept it with a hand-drawn electronic signature. You'll see the acceptance status and signature in your dashboard.
Which currencies are supported? ▾
ProposalKit supports 13+ currencies including INR, USD, EUR, GBP, AUD, CAD, SGD, AED, JPY, CNY, SEK, CHF, and NZD. Each currency formats correctly with proper locale and symbol placement.
Can I use my own branding? ▾
Absolutely. Upload your company logo, pick a brand color, and choose a font. With White-Label mode, you can even replace the ProposalKit name with your own company name throughout the app and PDFs.
How does the AI assistant work? ▾
The AI Writing Assistant is powered by Anthropic's Claude API. You bring your own API key (entered in Settings). It can improve, expand, shorten, or fix grammar in any proposal section. Custom prompts are also supported.
Is there a mobile app? ▾
ProposalKit works on any device with a modern browser. On mobile, you can install it as a PWA from your browser's "Add to Home Screen" option for a native-like experience with offline support.
