FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is ProposalKit really free?
Yes. The Free plan includes unlimited proposals, all 13 PDF templates, client management, payment tracking, analytics, and offline support. No signup, no credit card.
Where is my data stored?
All data is stored locally in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server. You can export and import your data as JSON at any time from Settings.
Does it work offline?
Yes. ProposalKit is a Progressive Web App with full offline support. Create, edit, and export proposals without an internet connection.
Can clients sign proposals digitally?
Yes. Share a secure link and clients can view the proposal in a clean portal and accept with a hand-drawn electronic signature.
Which currencies are supported?
13+ currencies including INR, USD, EUR, GBP, AUD, CAD, SGD, AED, JPY, CNY, SEK, CHF, and NZD. Each formats correctly with proper locale and symbol.
Can I use my own branding?
Upload your logo, pick a brand color, and with White-Label mode replace the ProposalKit name with your own throughout the app and PDFs.
How does the AI assistant work?
Powered by Anthropic's Claude API. Bring your own API key. It can improve, expand, shorten, or fix grammar in any section. Custom prompts supported.
Is there a mobile app?
ProposalKit works on any device with a modern browser. Install it as a PWA from "Add to Home Screen" for a native-like experience.
